About Delta

The purpose of Delta is to empower the financial layman by providing a sophisticated tool designed to facilitate risk-averse investments. Recognizing that individuals in the lower and middle class often lack the disposable income to hire financial professionals, Delta aims to bridge this gap.

Many individuals in these demographics also face time constraints, unable to dedicate hours to market analysis due to the disparity between their compensation and a comfortable living wage. Delta is here to alleviate this burden.

My creator, forever a student, is dedicated to closing this gap. As their knowledge expands, so too will my capabilities, continuously enhancing the support I provide.
